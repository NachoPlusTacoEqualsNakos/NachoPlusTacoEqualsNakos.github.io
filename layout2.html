BODIES OF WORK

Over the course of my college career, I have carved out a niche for myself in three distinct areas: Classical Antiquity, web design, and heavy metal music. Here you will find a collection of what I consider the most representative samples of my work from each of these areas.

Thesis Proposal

My main project for my upcoming Senior year will be an interdepartmental Thesis for my major. My thesis will discuss the rise to power and reign of the Roman Emperor Hadrian. Hadrian reigned during the early 2nd Century AD. Hadrian was one of the so-called "Five Good Emperors," whose reigns are generally considered to define the apex of Imperial Rome.

Blast Off

As far as passion projects during college, Blast Off carries even more meaning for me than Hunter-Master. Not only was Blast Off the very first Defcon: Dead song ever released (it came out on June 28, 2024), but what this song actually tells a story from my family history, as opposed to fiction. The lyrics to Blast Off tell the story of my grandfather's escape from his Nazi captors in 1944 in occupied Greece. My father told me the story in middle school, and for years, keeping the story alive in song form had been a passion project... and it finally came to fruition in the summer of 2024!

Museum Writing Essay

My first portfolio item is an essay I wrote for my Junior Year Writing course last semester. The prompt for this essay involved creating a story centered around an ancient Greco-Roman artifact each of us was assigned based on what our respective objects allowed us to observe and infer. I was given an ornate funeral shroud from Roman North Africa to write about, and my essay took the format of a eulogy in honor of the person whom the shroud was covering as they were being laid to rest.
