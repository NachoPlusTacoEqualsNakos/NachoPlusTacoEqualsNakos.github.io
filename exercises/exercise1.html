My name is Nakos Maroudas, which is pronounced NAH-ko Muh-ROO-das.

Interests and Hobbies

My main academic interest is Classical Studies, which for those who don't know is the study of the Ancient Mediterranean world (primarily Ancient Greece and Rome). At UMass Amherst, this discipline is divided into four track. The track I'm on is the Ancient Languages track, meaning that the majority of my coursework entails reading and translating Ancient texts in their original languages. Ever since I was in middle school, I've had a strong passion for languages (Latin in particular), and my studies at UMass have only made that passion stronger

In my free time, I enjoy lots of different activities. I love creative writing (song- writing and poetry especially), watching basketball games, hitting the gym with friends, playing guitar (more on that below), watching movies, and doing voice impressions. Particularly during spooky season and the winter, one of my go-to pastimes is watching old horror movies from as far back as the 1930s.

Extracurricular Pursuits

In addition to Classics, my other major passion in life is music. I like many different kinds of music, but my absolute favorite genre is heavy metal. My favorite metal band of all time is Testament, and I also really like Sabaton, Exodus, and The Black Dahlia Murder (among many, many others!)

In fact, I play guitar in a metal band of my own! In November of 2023, I founded a thrash metal band called Defcon: Dead. I put the band together along with some friends of mine from UMass. We've played a handful of shows so far, and are currently working on our debut EP, which will be available on all streaming platforms by mid-spring or so! Check out our website!

Skills and Proficiencies

Over the course of almost a decade of learning, I have gained varying degrees of fluency in several foreign languages such as Latin, Modern Greek, and Swedish. After taking to Latin fairly quickly in middle and high school, I really capitalized on my interest in language once I arrived at college.
