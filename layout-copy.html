Hey There, Friends!

My name is Nakos Maroudas (pronounced NAH-ko Muh-ROO-das). I am a Junior at UMass Amherst, where I am currently studying Classics. In addition to my Bachelor's studies - which primarily concerns the languages, history, and literature of the Ancient Greco-Roman world - I am an aspiring web developer, as well as an up-and-coming heavy metal musician.

Outside of school, I play lead guitar in a thrash metal which I founded with some UMass friends. We are called Defcon: Dead. Thrash metal is my favorite music in the world, and my ambition with Defcon: Dead is for it to ultimately be what I do for a living.

But, of course, to quote the immortal AC/DC lyrics: "it's a long way to the top if you wanna rock 'n' roll." Knowing this, I made the decision coming into college to not only work towards my degree, but to use my time as an undergrad to hone other skillsets outside of music that can allow me to support myself in my ultimate pursuit of professional heavy metal music.
